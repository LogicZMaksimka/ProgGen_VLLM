A named location entity refers to the geographical location or relative proximity of the restaurant. Proximity descriptors such as "nearest", "nearby", "closest" and "local" are also named location entities.
Longer proximity descriptors such as "adjacent to a bar" are also named location entities.
A named location entity should include all relevant terms such as "in this city" as opposed to "city".

A named location entity must be the name of a location. Locations in mythology such as "Underworld" are also named location entities.
General reference to a location such as "city" and "business park" is not a named entity.
Demonyms such as "French", "English", "American", "African", "Australian", "Indian" and "Greek" are not relevant named entities. Viruses such as "COVID-19" are also not relevant named entities. Hurricanes and other natural disasters are not named location entities.

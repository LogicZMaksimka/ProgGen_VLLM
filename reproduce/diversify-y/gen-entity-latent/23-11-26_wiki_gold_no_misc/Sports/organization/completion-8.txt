3. Professional Golfers' Association (PGA) - Organization for professional golfers

6. International Association of Athletics Federations (IAAF) - International governing body for the sport of athletics
7. World Anti-Doping Agency (WADA) - Organization aimed at promoting, coordinating and monitoring the fight against doping in sports

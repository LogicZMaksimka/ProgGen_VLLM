1. Mount Everest - The highest mountain in the world, located in the Himalayas on the border between Nepal and China.
2. Amazon Rainforest - The largest tropical rainforest in the world, located in South America, primarily in Brazil.
3. Great Barrier Reef - The world's largest coral reef system, located in the Coral Sea off the coast of Queensland, Australia.
4. Sahara Desert - The largest hot desert in the world, located in North Africa.
5. Yellowstone National Park - A national park located primarily in the U.S. state of Wyoming, known for its geothermal features and wildlife.
6. Machu Picchu - An ancient Incan city located in the Andes mountains in Peru.
7. The Great Wall of China - A series of fortifications made of stone, brick, tamped earth, wood, and other materials, located in northern China.
8. The Grand Canyon - A steep-sided canyon carved by the Colorado River in the state of Arizona, United States.

1. United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) - a specialized agency of the United Nations aimed at promoting world peace and security through international cooperation in education, science, and culture.
2. National Geographic Society - a non-profit scientific and educational organization dedicated to exploring and protecting the planet.
3. Royal Geographical Society - a learned society in the United Kingdom founded for the advancement of geographical science.
4. International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN) - an international organization working in the field of nature conservation and sustainable use of natural resources.
5. National Park Service - an agency of the United States federal government that manages all national parks, many national monuments, and other conservation and historical properties.
6. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) - an independent executive agency of the United States federal government tasked with environmental protection matters.
7. Sierra Club - an environmental organization in the United States dedicated to exploring, enjoying, and protecting the planet.
8. World Wildlife Fund (WWF) - an international non-governmental organization working in the field of wilderness preservation and the reduction of human impact on the environment.

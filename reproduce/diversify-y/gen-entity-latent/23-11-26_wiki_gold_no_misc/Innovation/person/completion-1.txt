1. Elon Musk - CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, known for his innovative approach to technology and business.
2. Marie Curie - Nobel Prize-winning physicist and chemist who made groundbreaking discoveries in the field of radioactivity.
3. Steve Jobs - Co-founder of Apple Inc. and a pioneer in the field of personal computing and consumer electronics.
4. Ada Lovelace - Often considered the world's first computer programmer, her work laid the foundation for modern computing.
5. Thomas Edison - Inventor and entrepreneur known for his numerous contributions to the development of electrical power, telecommunications, and motion pictures.
6. Grace Hopper - Computer scientist and naval officer who was a pioneer in the development of computer programming languages and the early days of computing.
7. Alexander Graham Bell - Inventor of the telephone and a key figure in the development of communication technology.
8. Tim Berners-Lee - Inventor of the World Wide Web and a leading advocate for open access to information and technology.

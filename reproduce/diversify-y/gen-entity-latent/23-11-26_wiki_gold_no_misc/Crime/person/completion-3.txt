1. Al Capone - An American gangster and crime boss who attained notoriety during the Prohibition era.
2. Bonnie and Clyde - A notorious criminal couple who were active during the Great Depression, known for their bank robberies.
3. Ted Bundy - An American serial killer and necrophile who confessed to murdering 30 women during the 1970s.
4. Pablo Escobar - A Colombian drug lord and founder of the Medellín Cartel, one of the most powerful criminal organizations in history.
5. Jack the Ripper - An unidentified serial killer who terrorized the Whitechapel district of London in 1888, targeting female prostitutes.
6. Myra Hindley - An English woman who, along with her partner Ian Brady, committed the "Moors Murders" of five children in the 1960s.
7. John Dillinger - An American bank robber during the Great Depression, known for his daring escapes and public persona.
8. El Chapo - Joaquín "El Chapo" Guzmán, a Mexican drug lord and leader of the Sinaloa Cartel, one of the most powerful drug trafficking organizations in the world.

2. B.F. Skinner - American psychologist and behaviorist known for his work in operant conditioning
3. Carl Jung - Swiss psychiatrist and founder of analytical psychology
4. William James - American philosopher and psychologist, often referred to as the "father of American psychology"

6. Alfred Adler - Austrian medical doctor and psychotherapist, founder of individual psychology

8. Erik Erikson - German-American developmental psychologist known for his theory of psychosocial development

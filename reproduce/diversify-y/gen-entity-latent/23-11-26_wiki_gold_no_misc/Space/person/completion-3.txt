1. Valentina Tereshkova - the first woman to fly in space, she was a Soviet cosmonaut who flew in Vostok 6 in 1963.
2. Yuri Gagarin - a Soviet astronaut and the first human to journey into space, completing one orbit of the Earth in Vostok 1 in 1961.
3. Neil Armstrong - an American astronaut and the first person to walk on the Moon during the Apollo 11 mission in 1969.
4. Sally Ride - the first American woman to fly in space, she was a NASA astronaut and flew on the Space Shuttle Challenger in 1983.
5. Mae Jemison - the first African American woman to travel in space, she was a NASA astronaut and flew on the Space Shuttle Endeavour in 1992.
6. Buzz Aldrin - an American astronaut who was the second person to walk on the Moon during the Apollo 11 mission in 1969.
7. Christa McAuliffe - an American teacher who was selected to be the first civilian in space, but tragically died in the Space Shuttle Challenger disaster in 1986.
8. Alan Shepard - the first American in space, he flew in the Freedom 7 spacecraft in 1961 as part of the Mercury program.

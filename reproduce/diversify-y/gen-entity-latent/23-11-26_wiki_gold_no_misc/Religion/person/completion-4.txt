1. Pope Francis - The current head of the Roman Catholic Church and sovereign of the Vatican City State.

2. Dalai Lama - The spiritual leader of Tibetan Buddhism, believed to be the incarnation of a line of Tulkus.

3. Mother Teresa - A Roman Catholic nun and missionary who dedicated her life to helping the poor and sick in Calcutta, India.

4. Martin Luther - A German professor of theology, composer, priest, and monk who initiated the Protestant Reformation.

5. Rumi - A 13th-century Persian poet, jurist, Islamic scholar, theologian, and Sufi mystic.

6. L. Ron Hubbard - The founder of the Church of Scientology and the author of Dianetics: The Modern Science of Mental Health.

7. Guru Nanak - The founder of Sikhism and the first of the ten Sikh Gurus.

8. Joseph Smith - The founder of the Latter Day Saint movement and the first president of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

1. Mecca - A city in Saudi Arabia that is considered the holiest city in Islam
2. Jerusalem - A city in Israel that holds religious significance in Judaism, Christianity, and Islam
3. Vatican City - An independent city-state enclaved within Rome, Italy and the headquarters of the Roman Catholic Church
4. Varanasi - A city in India that is one of the holiest in Hinduism
5. Lourdes - A town in France that is a major pilgrimage site for Catholics due to reported apparitions of the Virgin Mary
6. Bodh Gaya - A city in India where the Buddha is said to have attained enlightenment, an important pilgrimage site for Buddhists
7. Mount Sinai - A mountain in the Sinai Peninsula in Egypt that is mentioned in the Bible as the place where Moses received the Ten Commandments
8. Ayodhya - A city in India that is important in Hindu mythology and is the birthplace of Lord Rama, a major figure in Hinduism.

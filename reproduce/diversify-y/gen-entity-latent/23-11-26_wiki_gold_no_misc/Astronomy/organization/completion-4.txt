1. European Space Agency (ESA) - an intergovernmental organization dedicated to the exploration of space
2. National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) - the United States government agency responsible for the civilian space program and aeronautics research
3. International Astronomical Union (IAU) - an organization of professional astronomers that is responsible for naming celestial objects and defining their characteristics
4. European Southern Observatory (ESO) - an intergovernmental research organization for astronomy, including ground-based astronomical observatories in Chile
5. SETI Institute - a non-profit research organization that explores the possibility of life beyond Earth
6. Royal Astronomical Society (RAS) - a learned society and membership organization dedicated to promoting the study of astronomy and geophysics
7. International Space Station Program - a multi-national collaborative project involving space agencies from Russia, the United States, Europe, Japan, and Canada
8. Keck Observatory - a two-telescope astronomical observatory located on the summit of Mauna Kea in Hawaii

1. Mount Wilson Observatory - A historic observatory located in the San Gabriel Mountains near Pasadena, California, USA.

2. Mauna Kea Observatory - A collection of astronomical observatories located on the summit of Mauna Kea on the Big Island of Hawaii, USA.

3. Paranal Observatory - An astronomical observatory located in the Atacama Desert of northern Chile.

4. Arecibo Observatory - A large radio telescope located near the town of Arecibo in Puerto Rico.

5. European Southern Observatory - An intergovernmental research organization for astronomy, located in Garching near Munich, Germany.

6. Green Bank Observatory - An astronomical observatory located in Green Bank, West Virginia, USA.

7. Siding Spring Observatory - An astronomical observatory located near Coonabarabran, New South Wales, Australia.

8. La Silla Observatory - An astronomical observatory located in the southern Atacama Desert of Chile.

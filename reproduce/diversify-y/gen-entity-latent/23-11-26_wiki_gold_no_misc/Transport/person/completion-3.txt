1. Elon Musk - CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, known for his contributions to the development of electric vehicles and space travel.
2. Amelia Earhart - American aviation pioneer and the first female aviator to fly solo across the Atlantic Ocean.
3. Ferdinand Porsche - Founder of the Porsche car company and designer of the Volkswagen Beetle.
4. Orville and Wilbur Wright - American aviation pioneers credited with inventing and building the world's first successful airplane.
5. Malcolm McLean - American businessman and the "father of containerization," revolutionizing global trade with the invention of the shipping container.
6. T. E. Lawrence - British military officer and diplomat known for his role in the Arab Revolt during World War I and his involvement in the development of the armoured car for desert warfare.
7. Bonnie and Clyde - American criminal couple known for their bank robberies and escape from the police in stolen cars during the Great Depression.
8. Margaret Hamilton - American computer scientist and systems engineer who led the team that developed the onboard flight software for NASA's Apollo space program.

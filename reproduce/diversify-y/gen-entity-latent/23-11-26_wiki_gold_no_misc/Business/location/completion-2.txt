1. Wall Street - A well-known financial district in New York City, United States.
2. Silicon Valley - A region in the southern part of the San Francisco Bay Area in Northern California, United States, known for its technology and startup culture.
3. The City of London - A historic financial district in London, United Kingdom.
4. Tokyo Stock Exchange - One of the largest stock exchanges in the world, located in Tokyo, Japan.
5. Hong Kong Stock Exchange - A stock exchange based in Hong Kong, China.
6. Frankfurt Stock Exchange - One of the world's largest stock exchanges, located in Frankfurt, Germany.
7. Mumbai - The financial capital of India, home to the Bombay Stock Exchange and the National Stock Exchange of India.
8. Toronto Stock Exchange - The largest stock exchange in Canada, located in Toronto, Ontario.

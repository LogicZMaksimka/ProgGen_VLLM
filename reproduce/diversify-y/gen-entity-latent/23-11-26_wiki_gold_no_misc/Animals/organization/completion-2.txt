1. World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF) - an international non-governmental organization for the conservation, research and restoration of the environment, formerly named the World Wildlife Fund.
2. The Nature Conservancy - a charitable environmental organization, working to preserve the diversity of life on Earth by protecting crucial habitats around the world.
3. International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN) - an international organization working in the field of nature conservation and sustainable use of natural resources.
4. Wildlife Conservation Society (WCS) - a global organization committed to promoting the long-term conservation of wildlife and the ecosystems upon which they depend.
5. Royal Society for the Protection of Birds (RSPB) - a charitable organization that works to protect birds and the wider environment through conservation, public education, and advocacy.
6. Humane Society International (HSI) - an international animal protection organization that works to protect all animals, including animals in laboratories, farm animals, companion animals, and wildlife.
7. Sea Shepherd Conservation Society - a non-profit, marine wildlife conservation organization that uses direct-action tactics to protect marine life.
8. Defenders of Wildlife - a conservation organization working to protect and restore imperiled wildlife and their habitats in North America.

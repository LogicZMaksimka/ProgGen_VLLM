1. Elon Musk - CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, known for his work in developing electric vehicles and space transportation.
2. Amelia Earhart - Aviation pioneer and the first female aviator to fly solo across the Atlantic Ocean.
3. Ferdinand Magellan - Portuguese explorer who led the first expedition to circumnavigate the globe by sea.
4. Karl Benz - German engineer and inventor of the first automobile powered by an internal combustion engine.
5. Wilbur and Orville Wright - American aviation pioneers credited with inventing, building, and flying the world's first successful motor-operated airplane.
6. Grace Hopper - American computer scientist and United States Navy rear admiral who was a pioneer in developing computer programming languages and technology for transportation systems.
7. Malcolm McLean - American entrepreneur and inventor of the shipping container, revolutionizing the transportation of goods by sea.
8. Shirley Chisholm - The first African American woman elected to the US Congress and a notable advocate for transportation infrastructure and public transit.

1. Cambridge University - A well-known location for mathematics research and education, home to the renowned Isaac Newton Institute for Mathematical Sciences.

2. Princeton, New Jersey - Location of the Institute for Advanced Study, which has been home to many influential mathematicians, including Albert Einstein and John von Neumann.

3. Moscow, Russia - Known for its strong tradition in mathematical research, with institutions such as the Steklov Institute and the Independent University of Moscow.

4. Oberwolfach, Germany - Home to the Mathematisches Forschungsinstitut Oberwolfach, a leading research center in mathematics.

5. Kyoto University - A prestigious institution in Japan with a strong mathematics department and research programs.

6. Paris, France - Location of the Institut des Hautes Études Scientifiques, a prominent research institute in mathematics.

7. Zürich, Switzerland - Home to ETH Zurich, one of the leading universities for mathematics research and education in the world.

8. Bengaluru, India - Known for its thriving mathematics community, with institutions such as the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research and the Indian Institute of Science.

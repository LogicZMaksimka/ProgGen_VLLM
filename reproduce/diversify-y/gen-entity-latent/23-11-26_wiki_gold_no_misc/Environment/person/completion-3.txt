1. Greta Thunberg - A Swedish environmental activist who has gained international recognition for her efforts to combat climate change.

2. David Attenborough - An English broadcaster and natural historian known for his work in promoting environmental conservation and sustainability.

3. Rachel Carson - An American marine biologist and conservationist whose book "Silent Spring" is credited with sparking the modern environmental movement.

4. Wangari Maathai - A Kenyan environmental activist and Nobel Peace Prize laureate known for founding the Green Belt Movement, which focuses on tree planting, conservation, and women's rights.

5. Al Gore - An American politician and environmentalist who has been a leading advocate for action on climate change through his work on projects like the documentary "An Inconvenient Truth."

6. Vandana Shiva - An Indian environmental activist and author who has been a prominent advocate for sustainable agriculture, biodiversity, and social justice.

7. Wang Suya - A Chinese environmental activist known for her work on air pollution and for her efforts to raise awareness about environmental issues in China.

8. Severn Cullis-Suzuki - A Canadian environmental activist who became known for her speech at the Rio Earth Summit in 1992, where she demanded action on behalf of future generations.

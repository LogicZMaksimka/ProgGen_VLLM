1. World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF) - An international non-governmental organization working on issues regarding the conservation, research, and restoration of the environment.
2. Greenpeace - A non-governmental environmental organization focused on global environmental issues such as deforestation, overfishing, and climate change.
3. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) - An agency of the United States federal government focused on protecting human health and the environment.
4. United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) - An agency of the United Nations that coordinates environmental activities, assisting in the development and implementation of environmental policies and practices.
5. National Audubon Society - A non-profit environmental organization dedicated to the conservation of birds and their habitats.
6. Sierra Club - An environmental organization in the United States focused on promoting and protecting the environment.
7. Natural Resources Defense Council (NRDC) - A non-profit international environmental advocacy group with a mission to safeguard the Earth: its people, its plants and animals, and the natural systems on which all life depends.
8. Friends of the Earth - An international network of environmental organizations in 76 countries.

6. Arctic Circle - The Arctic Circle is one of the five major circles of latitude that mark maps of the Earth, and it is the northernmost of these circles.
7. Sahara Desert - The Sahara Desert is a desert located on the African continent, and it is the largest hot desert in the world.
8. Chesapeake Bay - Chesapeake Bay is an estuary in the U.S. states of Maryland and Virginia, and it is the largest estuary in the United States.

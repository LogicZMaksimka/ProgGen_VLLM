1. Neil Armstrong - American astronaut and the first person to walk on the moon during the Apollo 11 mission in 1969. 

2. Valentina Tereshkova - Soviet cosmonaut and the first woman to fly in space, orbiting the Earth 48 times aboard Vostok 6 in 1963.

3. Elon Musk - CEO and lead designer of SpaceX, a private aerospace manufacturer and space transportation company.

4. Sally Ride - American astronaut and physicist who became the first American woman in space when she flew aboard the Space Shuttle Challenger in 1983.

5. Yuri Gagarin - Soviet cosmonaut and the first human to journey into outer space, orbiting the Earth aboard Vostok 1 in 1961.

6. John Glenn - American astronaut and former U.S. senator who became the first American to orbit the Earth in 1962 aboard the Friendship 7 spacecraft.

7. Mae Jemison - American astronaut and physician who became the first African American woman to travel in space aboard the Space Shuttle Endeavour in 1992.

8. Chris Hadfield - Canadian astronaut and former commander of the International Space Station who gained popularity for his social media presence and musical performances in space.

1. European Space Agency (ESA) - an intergovernmental organization dedicated to the exploration of space.

2. SpaceX - a private aerospace manufacturer and space transportation company founded by Elon Musk.

3. National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) - the United States government agency responsible for the nation's civilian space program and for aeronautics and aerospace research.

4. Russian Federal Space Agency (Roscosmos) - the governmental body responsible for the space science program of the Russian Federation.

5. Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) - the space agency of the Government of India, responsible for the country's space research and exploration program.

6. Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA) - the national aerospace agency of Japan, responsible for research, technology development, and the launch of satellites into orbit.

7. China National Space Administration (CNSA) - the national space agency of China, responsible for the country's space program and for planning and development of space activities.

8. European Space Research and Technology Centre (ESTEC) - the European Space Agency's main technology development and test center for spacecraft and space technology.

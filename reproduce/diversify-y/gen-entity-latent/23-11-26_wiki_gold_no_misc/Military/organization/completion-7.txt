1. Special Air Service (SAS) - A special forces unit of the British Army known for its counter-terrorism and special reconnaissance operations. 
2. United States Special Operations Command (USSOCOM) - A unified combatant command of the U.S. Department of Defense responsible for overseeing special operations forces. 
3. Russian Aerospace Forces - The branch of the Russian military responsible for air and space operations. 
4. Royal Navy - The naval warfare service branch of the British Armed Forces. 
5. People's Liberation Army (PLA) - The armed forces of the People's Republic of China, consisting of ground, naval, and air forces. 
6. United States Marine Corps (USMC) - A branch of the United States Armed Forces responsible for conducting expeditionary and amphibious operations. 
7. Israeli Defense Forces (IDF) - The military forces of the State of Israel, responsible for defense and security. 
8. French Foreign Legion - A branch of the French Army which allows foreign recruits to serve in its ranks.

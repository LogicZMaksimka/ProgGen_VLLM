1. International Air Transport Association (IATA) - a trade association for the world's airlines, representing about 290 airlines comprising 82% of global air traffic.

2. Booking.com - a travel fare aggregator and travel metasearch engine for lodging reservations

3. Lonely Planet - a large travel guide book publisher

4. National Geographic Society - a global nonprofit organization committed to exploring and protecting our planet

5. Airbnb - an online marketplace for lodging, primarily homestays for vacation rentals, and tourism activities

6. International Federation of Tour Guides (IFTG) - an organization representing professional tour guides worldwide

7. American Automobile Association (AAA) - a federation of motor clubs throughout North America providing roadside assistance, travel services, and a variety of discounts

8. International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN) - a membership union composed of both government and civil society organizations. They aim to influence, encourage, and assist societies around the world to conserve the integrity and diversity of nature.
